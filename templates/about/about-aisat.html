Institution Overview

Excellence since 2002

Albertian Institute of Science and Technology (AISAT) is an autonomous engineering college located at Kalamassery, Ernakulam, Kerala. Established in 2002 by the Diocese of Ernakulam-Angamaly, the institution is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE.

AISAT is known for its strong emphasis on academic excellence, research culture, industry collaboration, and holistic student development. The campus spans over 15 acres with modern infrastructure including smart classrooms, research laboratories, a well-stocked central library, and sports facilities.
