About CSE

Established in 2000, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at AISAT has been at the forefront of technical education in Kerala, nurturing innovation, research, and industry-ready talent for over two decades.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in Computer Science education and research, producing globally competent engineers who are ethically grounded and socially responsible, capable of driving digital transformation across industries.

{# Mission #}

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education through an innovative curriculum, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and industry-academia collaboration. We are committed to fostering research, creativity, and entrepreneurship among our students and faculty.
